Who We Are

We are a small family-owned restaurant local to Fresno. We pride ourselves in offering fresh homestyle Khmer cuisine native to Cambodia. Our services include dine-in, carryout, and even catering. Opened since July 2016, we also host special events and Khmer Workshops. Feel free to checkout our Facebook and Instagram feeds for details on upcoming events and new specials!
